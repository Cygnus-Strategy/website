We hold the security and privacy of our clients in the highest regard. Maintaining the confidentiality and integrity of all data that we handle is paramount.

Our consultancy operates within a robust and proactive information security framework designed in alignment with NIST recommendations. All information, whether electronic or physical, is secured through multiple layers of protective measures that include encryption, secure networks, and advanced access control mechanisms. We adhere strictly to the principle of data minimization, collecting and processing only the information necessary during an engagement. Once the data lifecycle completes its course, we employ secure data disposal methods.

We will never share your information with anyone without your explicit permission. We will never sell your information to anyone.

We are happy to utilize client-provided hardware. We strongly prefer Unix or Unix-like operating systems (macOS, Linux, BSD), but we are happy to work with Windows systems as well.

A full copy of our information security and privacy policy is available by request.
